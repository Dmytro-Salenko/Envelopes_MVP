How to Print Wedding Envelopes at Home

Want to print beautiful wedding envelopes without expensive software or complicated tools? Here's a simple, step-by-step guide to create and print custom envelopes using Envelo.love — an online editor for editable and printable templates.

1. Choose Your Envelope Size

Most wedding envelopes are 7×5 inches. All templates at Envelo.love are pre-sized for this format and print easily on standard home printers.

2. Pick a Template

Visit the template catalog and choose a design that matches your wedding style — modern, script, or minimalist. All templates are editable in your browser.

3. Upload Your Address List

Use the downloadable CSV file to import your guest addresses. The system will automatically generate personalized envelopes for each guest using a mail merge system.

4. Customize Fonts and Layout

Use the online editor to adjust fonts, text size, spacing, or position. No installation or design knowledge required — everything works directly in your browser.

5. Export Your Design

Click “Save” to generate a high-resolution PDF, ready for print. You’ll receive a preview and a final print file with all addresses included.

6. Print at Home or at a Print Shop

You can print your envelopes at home using a standard inkjet or laser printer. Use thick, high-quality envelope paper (like 80 lb or 100 gsm) and set your printer to “envelope” mode or manually choose a 7×5 inch page size.

  • Use the rear feed tray if your printer has one
  • Choose “Best quality” or “Photo” mode in settings
  • Run a test print on regular paper first

Alternatively, you can take the generated PDF file to a local print shop or copy center. Since the file is already formatted for professional printing, you’ll just need to bring a USB drive or email it to the shop. Most printers will be able to handle the format with no issues.
