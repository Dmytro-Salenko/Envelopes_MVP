Privacy Policy

Envelo.love does not collect, store, or process any personal data from users directly. When you upload a CSV address file or customize a design, all data is processed securely by a third-party tool (PitchPrint) during your session. We do not store or retain any uploaded content on our servers.

We use PitchPrint to enable design customization. Your session data (such as uploaded names and addresses) is temporarily handled by PitchPrint and subject to their own privacy policies. You can read more at: https://pitchprint.com/privacy.

Envelo.love does not use your data for marketing, analytics, or tracking. We do not share your data with advertisers or third parties.

Cookies & LocalStorage

PitchPrint may use local storage and cookies to remember session preferences, uploaded images, or design changes. These are essential for the design editor to function properly. By using this site, you consent to this use.

Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the right to access, rectify, or delete any personal data held by third parties. If you have concerns, you may contact us at hello@envelo.love, and we will assist you in exercising your rights.
