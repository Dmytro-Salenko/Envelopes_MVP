Privacy Policy

Envelo.love is a simple design tool built with privacy in mind. We do not collect or store any personal data on our servers.

What We Process

When you use our design editor, the data you upload (such as names and addresses in a CSV file) is handled securely by our design partner, PitchPrint. They store and process your design temporarily to generate previews and print-ready files.

Third-Party Services

We use PitchPrint for design customization. They may process information such as names, addresses, and emails in order to generate design previews. You can read their privacy policy here.

Cookies & Analytics

We use privacy-friendly analytics (via Plausible.io) to understand general usage — we do not use cookies for tracking or advertising.

Your Rights

You may request to delete your data by contacting us at hello@envelo.love.

Questions?

We're a small indie team that values your trust. If you have any concerns about how your data is handled, please contact us.
