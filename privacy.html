Privacy Policy

At Envelo.love, your privacy matters to us. We do not store or collect any personal information directly on our servers. Our platform is designed to keep your data as secure and minimal as possible.

Data Handling

When you upload address lists (CSV files) or customize designs, your data is processed directly by our third-party design partner, PitchPrint. We do not have access to or store this information on our servers.

All processing occurs within the PitchPrint editor, which operates securely and complies with global privacy standards such as GDPR. You can read more about their data handling practices here:

https://pitchprint.com/privacy

What We Collect

We do not collect any personal data such as names, email addresses, or uploaded files. We use anonymous tracking tools (like basic analytics) only to understand general site usage.

Third-Party Services

All design processing is handled by PitchPrint. We rely on their infrastructure to deliver a smooth and responsive design experience. PitchPrint stores temporary project data on secure AWS servers located in the EU.

Your Consent

By using Envelo.love, you acknowledge that your data will be temporarily processed by a third-party service (PitchPrint) for the purpose of generating envelope designs. This data is not stored or reused by Envelo.love.

Questions?

If you have concerns or questions about our privacy practices, feel free to contact us at hello@envelo.love.
