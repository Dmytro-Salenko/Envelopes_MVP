Frequently Asked Questions

What is Envelo.love?

Envelo.love is a simple tool to help you design personalized envelope templates and generate print-ready PDFs — ideal for weddings, events, and personal mailings.

Do I need to create an account?

No. You don’t need to create an account or sign in — just upload your address list and start designing.

What format should my address list be in?

We support CSV files. You can download our template to make sure your file is formatted correctly.

Can I use different fonts and colors?

Yes! You can choose from a variety of font styles and colors when editing your envelope template.

Can I download my design as a PDF?

Yes. Once you're done customizing, you can download a high-quality, print-ready PDF.
