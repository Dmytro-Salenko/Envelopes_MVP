Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Envelo.love?

Envelo.love is a simple tool to create beautiful, personalized envelopes — perfect for weddings, showers, and special mailings. Upload your address list and get a print-ready PDF in minutes.

2. Do I need to install anything?

No. Everything runs in your browser. Just pick a template, upload a CSV file with addresses, and you're ready to go.
