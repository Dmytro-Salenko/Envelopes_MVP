Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Envelo.love?

Envelo.love is a simple tool to create beautiful, personalized envelopes — perfect for weddings, showers, and special mailings. Upload your address list and get a print-ready PDF in minutes.

2. Do I need to install anything?

No. Everything runs in your browser. Just pick a template, upload a CSV file with addresses, and you're ready to go.

3. Is it compatible with home printers?

Yes. You can download a high-quality PDF and print it at home or take it to a print shop.

4. How do I prepare my address list?

Download our CSV template and fill in your addresses. Make sure each row has a name, street, city, state, and ZIP code.

5. Can I use custom fonts or colors?

Yes! Our editor allows you to change fonts, colors, and layout elements. You have full creative control over the final design.

6. Is this free?

We’re currently in beta. All features are free to use while we test and improve the experience.

7. Who built this?

We’re a small indie project built with care — to help people create meaningful printed moments with ease.
